Pasta

Does it get any better than a pasta with tomato sauce and basil? Probably not. And in this version, you don't even need to pluck or chop the basil to infuse the sauce with all that flavor. After all, what on this earth goes together better than tomatoes and basil ?

Ingredients:

  • 100g Pasta
  • 50g Tomato sauce
  • 2-3 Leaf of basil

Steps:

  1. Boil 300ml of water.
  2. Put 100g of Pasta into the boiling water.
  3. Add salt into the boiling water.
  4. Depending on you pasta type the cooking time will be different. Check your pasta coocking time.
  5. Carefully pour the water from the pot
  6. Add tomato sauce and basil of your liking.
  7. Enjoy and don't forget to wash the pod and the dishes.
