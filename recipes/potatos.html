Baked potatoes

Crisp-skinned, fluffy jacket potatoes are a budget-friendly way to feed a crowd.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Potatoes
  • 1 Lemon
  • A little bit of pepper

Steps:

  1. Clean the potatoes and wash thoroughly.
  2. Cut the potatoes as you prefer.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C and put the potatoes for 40-50 minutes.
  4. After 20-30 minutes check them and flip on the other side to have a uniform texture.
  5. Take them out of the oven and put some pepper.
  6. Cut the lemon in slices.
  7. Enjoy.
