Chicken breast

Chicken breasts are famous for being a rich source of protein. It is a popular choice amongst athletes, people who are into fitness and diets. To avoid extra calories, you can opt for skinless chicken breasts. It is also rich in Vitamin A, Vitamin C and Vitamin D.

Ingredients:

  • 400g Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • 20g Oil

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken in slices.
  2. Coat the pan with the oil and put the sliced chicken.
  3. After some time rotate the chicken to have both sides cooked.
  4. Enjoy and don't forget to wash the pod and the dishes.
